THE idea

Our vison

Our vision

This project represents our hope, capacity, and ambition as Moroccan high schoolers, a unique way to fascinate the world with our ideas and inventions. We were able to design an electric ecological car, an experiment in developing a technology for the future.

The idea was insisted on by team leader Amine Mahdane, and the application was the result of a team effort in which a group of students with scientific abilities from different cities of Morocco participated in the making.

Goals

our goals and objectives.

The idea behind this project is to remind everyone about the important threats climate change and global warming put up to humanity by informing people about the fierce urgency of this situation and following up on the 13th and 9th SDGs while also showing what today's talented youngsters are capable of if provided with the suitable tools and knowledge..

IMPROVEMENTS

Future improvements.

SV1 will make for a perfect expiriment ground for future ideas and improvements. Among the possible future improvements of our project is the adoption of a drone-like system that allows the user to remotely control the car through a special interface, along with cruise control and other possible ideas we might add later on if provided with the required tools.
